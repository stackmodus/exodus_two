Our
Story

Our Vision

A society where everyone receives compassionate support and equitable treatment.

Established on May 25, 2020, Exodus T.W.O (The Way Out) is a non-profit organization under section 501(c) of the United States Internal Revenue Code. Our name is inspired by the biblical account from the Book of Exodus, drawing a parallel between the plights of ancient Israelites and individuals grappling with mental illness in the modern world.

That's where Exodus T.W.O steps in — offering a way out from the constraints and difficulties caused by mental illness. Through collaboration with various stakeholders, we facilitate access to timely counseling, legal advocacy, employment opportunities, and community integration.

Mission

Exodus T.W.O is dedicated to empowering individuals including adolescence and the incarcerated with mental illness and their families by providing comprehensive support, advocacy, and resources. We strive to break the barriers and stigmas surrounding mental health, offering a pathway to recovery.

What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive support across multiple areas to help individuals find their way out.

Mental Health Support

We offer immediate and compassionate mental health services — counseling, crisis intervention, and resources for those diagnosed with mental health disorders.

Addiction Recovery

Comprehensive treatment focusing on root causes — counseling, group therapy, and recovery management services.

Prison Programs

Advocacy for incarcerated individuals, working with court systems to ensure fair treatment and rehabilitation resources.

Homeless Support

Resettlement assistance to help individuals rebuild their lives with dignity and access to essentials.

Adolescent Services

Specialized programs for youth mental health and substance abuse with age-appropriate support.

Digital Equity

Bridging the digital divide to ensure underserved communities have access to technology and resources.
