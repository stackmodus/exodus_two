Who We Are

Exodus T.W.O (The Way Out) is a non-profit inspired by the Book of Exodus - drawing a parallel between the plights of ancient Israelites and individuals grappling with mental illness today. We offer a way out from the constraints caused by mental illness.

Through collaboration with various stakeholders, we facilitate access to counseling, legal advocacy, employment opportunities, and community integration.

Vision

A society where everyone receives compassionate support and equitable treatment.

Mission

Breaking barriers and stigmas, offering a pathway to recovery for all.

Our Services

Comprehensive support to help individuals find their way out.

Mental Health

Counseling, crisis intervention, and resources for those with mental health disorders.

Addiction Recovery

Root-cause treatment with counseling, group therapy, and recovery management.

Prison Programs

Advocacy for incarcerated individuals - fair treatment and rehabilitation resources.

Homeless Support

Resettlement assistance to rebuild lives with dignity and essential resources.

Youth Services

Specialized programs for adolescent mental health and substance abuse.

Digital Equity

Bridging the digital divide for underserved communities.
