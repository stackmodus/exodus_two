About Us

Who We Are

Established on May 25, 2020, Exodus T.W.O (The Way Out) is a non-profit organization under section 501(c) of the United States Internal Revenue Code. Our name is inspired by the biblical account from the Book of Exodus, drawing a parallel between the plights of ancient Israelites and individuals grappling with mental illness in the modern world.

That's where Exodus T.W.O steps in, offering a way out from the constraints and difficulties caused by mental illness.

Vision Statement

To create a society where individuals facing mental health challenges including adolescence, the incarcerated, and homelessness receive compassionate support, equitable treatment, and access to opportunities for recovery, leading to a fulfilled and inclusive life.

Mission Statement

Exodus T.W.O is dedicated to empowering individuals including adolescence and the incarcerated with mental illness and their families by providing comprehensive support, advocacy, and resources. We strive to break the barriers and stigmas surrounding mental health and incarceration, offering a pathway to recovery and a way out.

What We Do

Our Services

We provide comprehensive support across multiple areas to help individuals find their way out.

Mental Health Support

We offer immediate and compassionate mental health services for those diagnosed with various mental health disorders by directing them to where they can get immediate help.

Addiction & Substance Abuse

Our treatment program focuses on understanding the root causes of addiction and providing the necessary tools for recovery including counseling, group therapy, and recovery management.

Inmate & Prison Programs

We advocate for incarcerated individuals with mental health conditions, working with court systems and probation offices to ensure fair treatment and provide rehabilitation resources.

Homeless Support

Providing support and resettlement assistance for individuals experiencing homelessness, helping them rebuild their lives with dignity and access to essential resources.

Adolescent Services

Specialized programs addressing adolescent mental health and drug abuse, providing age-appropriate support and guidance for young people in need.

Digital Equity

Promoting digital equity and connectivity to ensure underserved communities have access to technology and the digital resources they need.

Together, We Can Make a Difference

Your support helps us provide essential services to those who need them most. Join our mission to bring hope and healing.
